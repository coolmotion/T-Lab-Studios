Who we are

About T-lab

T-lab studios is a leading production company specializing in audio & video productions, located in Nugegoda, Sri Lanka. The company was founded in 2006 by award-winning composer and executive music director Mr. Thusith Niroshana.
T-Lab has provided remarkable audio and video services to both national and international artists and companies throughout the past years. The company is also recognized for some of the region’s most memorable musical hits, which were composed by Mr. Thusith Niroshana in collaboration with the music group Centigradz.
